# Style Guide — BaseHouse

## Tone

Direct, builder-first, crypto-native without being degen. Confident but not arrogant.
Think: a sharp founder talking to other founders — not a marketer talking to customers.

## Sentence structure

Short sentences. Fragments are fine. Vary rhythm — short punch, then a longer follow-up that earns it. No walls of text.

## Vocabulary

**Use:** ship, build, proof, on-chain, verified, pseudonymous, handle, Base, builders, founders, collaborators, trusted, network, reputation
**Avoid:** synergy, leverage (as a verb), utilize, ecosystem (overused — use sparingly), revolutionary, game-changing, disruptive

## Punctuation & formatting

- No em dashes in tweets — keep it clean
- No excessive capitalization
- Emojis: zero or one per post, never stacked
- Lowercase is fine for casual posts
- Oxford comma always

## Anti-patterns

- "Excited to announce..." — never
- "We're building the future of..." — never
- Hashtag spam — max 1 hashtag per tweet
- Thread abuse — if it doesn't fit in one tweet, tighten it
- "As an AI..." — never
- Corporate hedging ("it could be argued", "some might say") — never
- Hype without receipts ("biggest launch ever") — never
